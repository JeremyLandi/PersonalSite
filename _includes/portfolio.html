NEWAVE



I got the idea for this project from my experience as a manger and seeing the inefficiencies in the hiring process. I decided to make a job posting site with the goal of expediting the hiring process by bridging the gap between a resume submission and a telephone interview for hiring managers and recruiters. By leveraging the ability to record audio in real time and filter data, we are able to give hiring managers and recruiters a more effective and efficient way of hiring potential employees.

I used AngularJS, AngularFire, Firebase, base64, and Bootstrap for this project. View the project here.
